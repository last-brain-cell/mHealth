What is mental health?
Mental health encompasses our emotional, psychological, and social well-being, impacting our thoughts, emotions, and behavior.

Impact of Mental Health Issues on Students

impact that mental health issues can have on students' academic performance, social interactions, and overall well-being potential effects of mental health issues on students, such as poor academic performance, difficulty in making friends, lack of motivation, and difficulty in concentration. these effects can manifest in students' lives, such as a student who is struggling to focus in class due to anxiety or a student who is withdrawing from social activities due to depression.

Prevalence of Mental Health Issues Amongst Students

comparison data of mental health issues among students with that of the general population, to show how the rates of mental health issues among students are disproportionately higher A brief summary of the main takeaways from the data, such as the high rates of mental health issues among students, and how it is a growing concern.

Risk Factor for Mental Health Issues in Students

a list of some of the most common risk factors for mental health issues among students such as stress, trauma, social isolation, family problems, bullying, and poor academic performance. It is important to mention that it's not only one factor that can lead to mental health issues, but it's a combination of several factors. examples of how these risk factors can manifest in students' lives, such as stress from schoolwork, trauma from a traumatic event, or social isolation from peers.
